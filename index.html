  • MVP scope definition

Product

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What technologies do you specialize in, and what services do you offer?

I work with full JavaScript ecosystem — React, Next.js, and TypeScript on the frontend, Node.js and Express on the backend, with PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and Supabase for databases. AI & LLM integrations (OpenAI, LangChain, RAG systems), and cloud infrastructure (AWS, Docker, CI/CD). My services includes building SaaS products, full stack web applications, business websites and AI Integration and Automation

How do we get started?

Simple — reach out via the contact form or email below. We'll have a quick call to align on your project goals, timeline, and budget. From there I'll put together a clear proposal covering scope, deliverables, and pricing. Once we're on the same page, we kick off immediately with no long waiting periods.

How long does a project usually take?

Timelines depend on scope. A landing page or brochure site usually takes 1–2 weeks. A full-stack web app or SaaS product typically takes 4–12 weeks, depending on features and complexity. I always provide a clear, realistic timeline upfront before starting and keep you updated throughout the entire process, so there are no surprises.

What does your development process look like?

I follow a structured five-phase approach — starting with product discovery and architecture design before writing code. Then full-stack development, followed by testing and optimization, and finally deployment and continuous iteration. Each phase has clear deliverables. Regular check-ins and demos make sure the product evolves in the direction you want.

Do you work with teams or just solo projects?

Both. I'm equally comfortable owning a project end to end as a solo developer, or plugging into an existing team as a frontend, backend, or full-stack contributor. I've collaborated with designers, product managers, and other engineers — remote async or real-time — and I adapt to whatever workflow and tools your team already uses.

Can you integrate AI into existing products?

Yes. I design and implement AI-powered features such as intelligent automation, AI assistants, document processing, recommendation systems, and data-driven workflows. The goal is to use AI where it actually adds value, not just as a trend.

Who do you typically work with?

Mostly early-stage startups building their first product, entrepreneurs with a SaaS idea ready to be brought to life, and small businesses that need a professional, high-performing web presence. I also work with agencies looking for reliable development support on client projects. If you have a real problem and want clean, working software — we'll get along fine.
